ğŸ“‹ Important Notes

Lion's Mane: Prefers consistent conditions, sensitive to temperature fluctuations. Benefits from good air circulation during fruiting.
Chestnut: Very adaptable variety, tolerates wider temperature ranges. Excellent for beginners.
Oyster: Most forgiving variety, grows in diverse conditions. Fast colonizer and reliable producer.
Cubensis: Requires higher temperatures and humidity. More sensitive to environmental changes than gourmet varieties.
Air Exchange: All varieties need fresh air exchange during fruiting (4-6 air changes per hour minimum).
Lighting: Indirect light (12-hour cycle) triggers pinning in most varieties. Avoid direct sunlight.
